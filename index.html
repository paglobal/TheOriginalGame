-Press Up Arrow Key to Move forward

-Press Down Arrow Key to Move backward

-Press Left Arrow Key to Rotate left

-Press Right Arrow Key Rotate right

-Press Spacebar to Shoot

-You can only fire 3 bullets at a time

-Use them wisely!!!

-Press Ctrl to Teleport

-The number of teleports you have are

indicated at the top left corner of the

game screen under your score

-The number of lives you have is indicated

by the number of triangles at the top left

corner of the game screen

-A white ring around you indicates that you are

invincible and so asteroids explode upon hitting you

-Press P to Play Or Pause

-Reload the page if the audio keeps breaking up
